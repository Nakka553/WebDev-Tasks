PLAYERS NFTs

The NFT Collection for players will be a set of 32 countries with 250 NFTs each, making a total of 8000 NFTs. The players will need these NFTs in order to participate in the MiniWorld cup games on a daily basis.

Every NFT Holder will have to pre-register for any of the 24 competitions that happen daily, compete with the other players and then the winner takes the big rewards for the day. The max number of players for each competition is 32.
